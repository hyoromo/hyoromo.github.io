Sound game training

Basic function

It is an application for practicing sound gate / rhythm game.
By the play the video, you can be pseudo play on it.

That there is in this app

  • transfer camera roll, from iTunes, play the video on YouTube
  • also Touch during movie playback will not be paused
  • adjust the playback start / end position of the moving image
  • adjust the scaling / display position of the video
  • adjust the playback speed of the video between 2.00 times from 0.25

How to register a video

  1. On the folder selection screen, select your favorite game folder or select "Add folder" from the bottom right
  2. On the movie selection screen, select "Choose movie" in the lower right, select the video import destination
  3. Register video from Youtube / In-app / Camera Roll
